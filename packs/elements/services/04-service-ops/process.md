New service stood up → defined, owned, mapped before go-live. Service change → mapping
updated. CI changes (from the `.03` packs) propagate into service impact automatically where
the mapping holds. Quarterly service review: owner/customer/criticality still right; mapping
complete. Mapping coverage % is the keystone health metric.

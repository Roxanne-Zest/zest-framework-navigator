- **Capability naming** — services named for the business capability they provide, never 1:1
  with an application or vendor. Defined with the business.
- **Accountability** — Service Owner accountable to a named Service Customer.
- **Criticality scheme** agreed (tiers) and applied consistently.
- **Scope:** critical services first; a service is in scope when its supporting CIs are at band 3.

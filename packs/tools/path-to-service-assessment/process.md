# Process — the engagement

Fixed shape: 10 working days of assessor effort across 2–3 elapsed weeks, one assessor.

| Day | Activity | Output |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Kickoff; confirm scope; request analyst-level read access and exports | Access checklist complete |
| 2–3 | Interviews: service owner(s), ops lead, CMDB/asset owner, one consumer | Interview notes; claimed scores |
| 3–5 | Instance inspection and data sampling per build.md | Evidence pack |
| 6 | Scoring session — settle contested cells | Scored grid v1 |
| 7 | Findings log drafted | Findings v1 |
| 8 | Outcome workshop with the catalogue | 2–4 outcomes agreed |
| 9 | Roadmap derivation (method.md) plus benefit baselining | Roadmap + baseline sheet |
| 10 | Playback: grid, findings, roadmap, next step | Signed-off assessment |

Roles: Zest assessor runs it; client sponsor is the decision-maker at the outcome workshop and playback; instance admin provides access and exports (about half a day total).

Failure modes and the rule:
- Access not granted by day 3 → pause the clock; never score blind
- Sponsor absent from the outcome workshop → rebook it; outcomes chosen by proxy do not stick
- New scope arrives mid-engagement → it goes in the next assessment, not this one

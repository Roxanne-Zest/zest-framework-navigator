# Commercials — the offer

Positioning: a fixed-fee assessment that ends with a costed, dependency-ordered delivery plan. The roadmap prices the follow-on work — the client buys certainty before they buy delivery.

Shape:
- Effort: 8–10 assessor days, 2–3 elapsed weeks
- Price: fixed fee at standard day rate × effort — [SET RATE]
- Prerequisites: analyst read access, export permission, sponsor availability on days 8 and 10

Deliverables, contracted:
1. Scored maturity grid with evidence references
2. Findings log
3. Outcome selection (2–4, from the catalogue)
4. Sequenced roadmap — packs, waves, effort
5. Benefit baseline sheet

What it sells next: wave 1 of the roadmap, quoted from the pack effort the roadmap already contains. No re-scoping gap between assessment and delivery — the assessment did the scoping.

Guarantee posture: every score is re-derivable from cited evidence. If the client shows an evidence error, the cell is re-scored without argument.

Not included: remediation, configuration changes, training. The assessment reads; the packs write.
